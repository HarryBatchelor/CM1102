Sir Clive Marles Sinclair was born 30th July 1940 in Richmond, London, England. He is an entrepreneur and inventor. His most famous work came in the late 1970s and early 1980s in the consumer electronic industry.
In 1961 he founded Sinclair Radionics where, in 1972, he made the Sinclair Executive, which is the first slim-line electronic pocket calculator. He later went onto produce the Sinclair ZX80 which was the UK's first mass-market home computerfor less than £100. Later on he founded Sinclair Research which went onto produce the ZX81 and the ZX Spectrum.
The ZX81 is often recognised as an important stepping stone in the early days of the British home computer industry
